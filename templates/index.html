Dura-Europos Annotation Tool

This tool is a fork of Wikidata Image Positions specifically meant for the annotation of Dura-Europos related images on Wikidata.

To get started first sign in with your Wikimedia unified account (any account associated with a Wikimedia project, such as Wikipedia or Wikidata) in the top right. Then check out the Dashboard to find an image you would like to annotate. You can make wikidata statements about what is depicted in the image, and then associate that statement with a region on the image (tutorial coming soon!). As a contributor, your annotations will be seen by project leads to give feedback or approve, which will post your annotations to wikidata. You will need a Wikimedia account in order to log in and annotate.

If you know what you want to annotate then search it up below by item id (starts with a Q).
